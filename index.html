My Projects

Unknown Escape is a first-person open world exploration game set in a mysterious scientific facility.

You have awakened in an unfamiliar place, contacted by an unknown person in need of your assistance...

Solve simple puzzles, explore the facility, collect items, and interact with the environment! All while piecing together a non-linear story.

This game was made as my high school graduation project. This is my largest project to this date.

Many thanks to my classmates for playtesting. Extra special thanks to Christer Eckermann from Snuti Games for extensive feedback, tips and support during development.

Dice Dungeon

Dice Dungeon is a short isometric puzzle game where you play as a die. Your goal is to complete the levels with as little moves as possible.

Each move changes the die's value sou you have to think about every move when solving puzzles.

This game is an entry to the GMTK Game Jam 2022 - theme Roll of the Dice.
